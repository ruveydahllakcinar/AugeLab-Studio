ABOUT US

AugeLab was founded in 2019 by an experienced team with such as image processing, artificial intelligence, industrial automation. AugeLab main focus is computer vision and artificial intelligence, enables even people who do not have technical expertise to develop solutions in this field with the platform it has developed.

Especially in industrial production areas, it is used in many sectors such as automotive, wood, textile, metal, machine manufacturing, industrial automation, and it significantly reduces commissioning costs due to the fact that it increases the production and quality efficiency of end users and enables them to develop in-house solutions.

On the other hand, which develops solutions with the Python coding language with AugeLab Studio Marketplace, enables companys, start-ups and freelance software developers to generate income by offering their solutions around a certain standard to end users, and offers the opportunity to reach more solutions constantly.
